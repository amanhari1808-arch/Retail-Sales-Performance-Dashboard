# Retail-Sales-Analytics-Dashboard-India (Interactive Dashboard creation using Power BI)
## Project Objective
The objective of this project is to analyze retail sales data using Power BI to uncover meaningful insights about sales performance, customer behaviour, and business trends. The dashboard focuses on evaluating key performance indicators such as total sales, total profit, total cost, average unit price, and total orders. It provides an interactive platform to explore patterns across product lines, customer segments, cities, payment methods, and time periods.

## Dataset Used
- <a href="https://github.com/amanhari1808-arch/Retail-Sales-Performance-Dashboard/blob/main/Retail%20Sales%20Dataset.xlsx">Dataset</a>

## Questions (KPIs)
- What is the total sales revenue generated?
- What is the total profit earned?
- What is the total number of orders placed?
- Which product line generates the highest sales?
- How does revenue vary by gender?
- What are the monthly sales trends?
- Which city contributes the most to total revenue?
- What is the most commonly used payment method?
- How does customer rating relate to sales performance?
- Which month recorded the highest sales
- Dashboard Interaction <a href="https://github.com/amanhari1808-arch/Retail-Sales-Performance-Dashboard/blob/main/Retail%20Sales%20Dashboard.png">View Dashboard</a>

## Process
- Imported the retail dataset into Microsoft Power BI Desktop.
- Cleaned the data using Power Query by handling missing values, removing duplicates, and correcting data types.
- Built a data model and established relationships between tables.
- Created DAX measures for KPIs such as total sales, total profit, average unit price, and total orders.
- Designed an interactive dashboard with KPI cards, bar charts, pie charts, line charts, and slicers for dynamic filtering.

## Project Insights
- Food and beverage products generate the highest sales among all product lines.
- Female customers contribute a larger share of revenue compared to male customers.
- Sales show fluctuations across months, with higher performance in the early months and a decline in mid-year.
- Major cities contribute differently to revenue, indicating location-based performance variation.
- Cash and digital payments (credit card and e-wallet) show varied usage across cities.
- Customer ratings tend to remain relatively stable, even when sales fluctuate.
- Gross income shows a gradual increasing trend over time, indicating steady profitability.

## Final Conclusion:
The Power BI dashboard provides a comprehensive and interactive view of retail sales performance. It highlights key drivers such as product line performance, customer segmentation, and seasonal trends.
These insights can help businesses improve decision-making in areas like inventory management, pricing strategies, customer targeting, and regional expansion. The use of Power BI enhances data visualization and enables efficient monitoring of retail operations, making it easier to identify trends and optimize business performance.
